Certificate Course in Big Data Analytic

The objective of this course is to provide the student with hands on experience in Big Data Analytics.
Duration : 320 Hrs      Course Fee : K----
Eligibility : Minimum of Grade 12 Certificate     NSQF Level : 7
Prerequisite : Fair knowledge of Computing Fundamentals and Fundamentals of Programming, Database Technology and Java Programming.
SL no Modules Hours
1 Big Data Fundamentals 30
2 Statics with R 90
3 Data Visualization - Analysis and Reporting 40
4 Business Analytics 60
5 Effective Communication and Soft Skills 60
6 Project 40

Outcome : The objective of Certificate Course in BigData Analytics (CCBDA) is to provide the student with an expertise Big data analytic domain. Analyze the big data using Statistics with R, Data Visualization - Analysis and Reporting, and Business Analytics. After the completion of the course, students can work in Statics with R, Data Visualization, Business Analytics
