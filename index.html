IT Education Center

A premier institute for training and enhancement of Information Technology skills funded by the Government of the Republic of India in collaboration with the Government of the Independent State of Papua New Guinea and operated in association with University of Papua New Guinea (UPNG) and the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing(CDAC).

Bilateral Project

The Centre of Excellence in Information Technology (CEIT) is an initiative of the Papua New Guinean and Indian Governments to bridge the gap between academia and industry and to create a pool of knowledgeable IT workers, generate employment opportunities by producing world-class IT professionals.
